August Events Highlight

Welcome Week AY24/25

During the first week of Semester 1 AY24/25, the Rotaract Club enthusiastically welcomed freshmen to join our vibrant community! We hosted an engaging booth where students could explore more about the club, its activities, and how to get involved. Our team was thrilled to meet the new students, share insights, and create a friendly atmosphere for those interested in becoming part of the Rotaract journey.


Welcome Tea

Rotaract Club's Welcome Tea on August 22nd was a key event kicking off the academic year AY24/25. The newly appointed president gave an inspiring speech, setting the stage for an exciting year. Directors from each division also shared insightful speeches, highlighting the diverse opportunities within the club. The event provided a valuable chance for freshmen interested in joining to connect with the club's leadership, fostering a strong sense of community and excitement for the year ahead.


Cardholder Workshop

On August 30th, the Rotaract Club of NTU hosted a creative cardholder design workshop at AIA Canopy, attracting participants eager to explore their artistic skills. This free event welcomed individuals from various age groups and provided a fantastic opportunity to design and create unique cardholders. Participants engaged in hands-on activities, allowing their creativity to shine while learning various design techniques.


Leadership Catalyst Bootcamp

The Professional Development Division organised a dynamic Leadership Catalyst Bootcamp on 31st August for the EXCOs and Directors of the Rotaract Club of NTU. This one-day event aimed to enhance leadership and communication skills while strengthening relationships for better cross-division collaboration. A special thank you to Prof. Rajaram, Senior Lecturer at NBS, for his insightful talk on leadership, conflict management, and teamwork. His insights are sure to positively influence our team culture.
