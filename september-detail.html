September Events Highlight

B.F.F Joy of Reading Launch Event

Embarking on September 3rd, Project B.F.F. Joy of Reading, a collaborative effort between the Youth Development and Professional Development divisions, is designed to ignite the academic passions of underprivileged primary students. The event featured a captivating hands-on terrarium workshop, where Rotaract members guided students in crafting miniature ecosystems to foster an interest in nature and sustainability.

Photography Workshop

The NTU Rotaract Club's Photography Workshop held on 5th September was a resounding success, filled with insightful techniques and valuable knowledge shared by passionate photography enthusiasts. The event received enthusiastic participation and contributions from both members and speakers, creating an immersive and fruitful experience for all attendees.

❮ ❯

Installation Night

The 39th Annual General Meeting and Installation Night on September 14 concluded successfully. Outgoing directors highlighted past achievements, providing valuable insights to new members. The dedication of all involved contributed to this success. We warmly welcome incoming directors and sub-committee members, anticipating a future filled with purposeful activities.
