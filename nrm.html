NRM

NTU Rotaract Month












NTU Rotaract Month (NRM) is a student-led initiative organised by the Rotaract Club of NTU, where multiple volunteering efforts along with professional development workshops and events are harmoniously held throughout a single month, highlighting the two main aspects of NTU Rotaract Club, which comprises of volunteering service and professional development. Main objectives of the project is to champion active volunteerism within the club, cultivate professional development opportunities and to catalyse collaborative initiatives.
