August Events Highlight

Welcome Week AY23/24

During the inaugural week of Semester 1 AY23/24, Rotaract Club warmly welcomed freshmen to join our vibrant community! We set up an engaging booth where students had the opportunity to learn more about Rotaract Club, its activities, and how they can become part of this exciting journey. Our team was delighted to connect with the incoming students, sharing information and fostering a welcoming environment for those interested in joining the club.


Welcome Tea

Rotaract Club's Welcome Tea, held on August 21st, marked a significant kickoff for the academic year AY23/24. The event featured an inspiring speech by our newly appointed president, setting the tone for an exciting year ahead. To provide valuable insights, directors from each division delivered impactful speeches, offering a glimpse into the diverse opportunities available within the club. The Welcome Tea served as a platform for freshmen keen on joining Rotaract to connect with the club's leadership, fostering a sense of community and enthusiasm for the upcoming year.


Leadership Catalyst Bootcamp

On the 19th of August, the Professional Development Division organized a one-day Leadership Catalyst Bootcamp for the EXCOs and Directors of Rotaract Club of NTU. The bootcamp serves to enhance the leadership and communication skills whilst also aim to strengthen the bond between the management committee for better cross-division cooperation.
