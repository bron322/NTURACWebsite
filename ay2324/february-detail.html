Serve with Your Heart workshop

The Serve with Your Heart workshop organized by the NTU Rotaract Club was an inspiring and hands-on event focused on community service and empathy. The workshop aimed to encourage participants to embrace the spirit of volunteerism by understanding the needs of various communities and how they can contribute meaningfully. Through interactive activities, group discussions, and real-life case studies, attendees were given insights into the importance of serving with compassion and empathy. The workshop also featured experienced speakers who shared their journeys in community service, motivating others to make a positive impact in society.


Web Design Workshop

The NTU Rotaract Club Web Design Workshop was a hands-on event aimed at empowering students with essential web development skills. Organized by the NTU Rotaract Club, the workshop introduced participants to the fundamentals of creating a website from scratch using popular web technologies. The event covered the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, offering a clear understanding of how to structure a webpage, apply styling, and incorporate interactive features.
